Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Built for Today

Ceylon Wood was founded with one simple belief — that furniture should tell a story. Every piece we craft is made using time-honoured Sri Lankan woodworking traditions, passed down through generations of master craftsmen in our workshop in Colombo.

We source only sustainably grown timber — Teak, Mahogany, Jak, and Rubber Wood — selecting each plank for its grain, strength, and beauty. No shortcuts, no compromises.
